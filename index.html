About Us

Our goal is to save more lives with your help.

Sixteen Foundation is a Community Organisation run by selfless volunteers. Our vision is to see communities around the world feel empowered through education. Our support ranges from the most deprived, war-torn villages to established organisations. Our mission is to enhance existing establishments to provide high quality education for each and every child. We simply believe that an educated community is one full of optimism for its future. Our dedication towards supporting educational causes lies in the legacy we would like to uphold for our late parents. With your relentless fundraising and donations we allow a child in education, dare to dream.
